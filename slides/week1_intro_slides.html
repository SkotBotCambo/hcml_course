Why do we study Machine Learning?

Get the class grounded in the ultimate purpose of this area of computer science. Students should be out of the headspace where the purpose is the "cool" factor of ML/AI/Data Science.

Who uses Machine Learning?

Start introducing the idea that * a data scientist is not necessarily a domain expert * a domain expert is not necessarily an end-user * an end-user is not necessarily the only person who is affected by the machine learning
